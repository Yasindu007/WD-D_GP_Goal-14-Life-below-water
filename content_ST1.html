Introduction to Life Below Water

The oceans cover more than 70% of the Earth's surface and are home to millions of species. UN Goal 14 aims to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources.

Threats to Marine Life

Marine ecosystems face numerous threats, including plastic pollution, overfishing, and climate change.

Actions We Can Take

Individuals can help by reducing plastic use, supporting sustainable seafood, and participating in beach cleanups.
