Marine Biodiversity Conservation Efforts by Country

Explore the unique marine animals and bioenvironments in different countries and the protocols they have implemented to protect these valuable ecosystems.

Country Unique Marine Animals/Bioenvironments Protocols to Save Them
Australia Great Barrier Reef, Dugong, Sea Turtles, Great White Shark Great Barrier Reef Marine Park Authority, No-take zones, Marine Protected Areas (MPAs), Coral restoration programs
Philippines Whale Sharks, Coral Reefs, Sea Turtles, Fish Species (e.g., Clownfish) Coral Triangle Initiative, Local Marine Sanctuaries, Community-Based Fisheries Management
Mexico Humpback Whales, Sea Lions, Manta Rays, Sharks, Baja California Marine Ecosystem Mexican Government's Marine Protected Areas, Sea Turtle Protection Program, Sustainable Fisheries Management
Brazil Giant Sea Turtles, Manatees, Amazon River Mouth Ecosystem Brazilian Marine Protected Areas, Coastal Regulations, NGOs focused on Marine Species Protection
South Africa Great White Shark, Southern Right Whale, Penguins, Cape Floral Region Marine Biodiversity Shark Conservation, Marine Protected Areas, Great White Shark Tagging Program, Anti-Poaching Laws
